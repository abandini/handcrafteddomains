Frequently Asked Questions

What fees do you charge?

We operate on a simple, transparent fee structure: a flat 9% success fee on the final sale price. There are no upfront costs to list your domain. The optional 'Bill-Boost' premium service is available for $49/year, which includes enhanced promotion, personalized sales outreach, and priority placement in our marketplace.

Is my listing exclusive to HandCraftedDomains?

No, we don't require exclusivity. You're free to list your domain with other brokers or marketplaces simultaneously. However, if you choose our 'Bill-Boost' premium service, we do ask for a 30-day exclusive listing period to maximize our marketing efforts.

How long does it take to get paid after a sale?

Once a sale is completed, funds are typically released from escrow within 3-5 business days after the domain transfer is confirmed. We use Escrow.com for all transactions, which ensures security for both buyers and sellers. The 9% success fee is automatically deducted from the final sale amount before the funds are released to you.

How do you verify domain ownership?

We use a simple TXT record verification process. After submitting your domain, you'll receive instructions to add a specific TXT record to your domain's DNS settings. This confirms you have control over the domain without requiring any transfer of ownership until an actual sale occurs.

What types of domains do you accept?

We focus on quality over quantity. We accept domains that have clear commercial value, strong branding potential, and are generally under 15 characters. We specialize in .com domains, but also accept premium domains with other extensions if they have exceptional branding potential. Each submission is reviewed by our team to ensure it meets our marketplace standards.
