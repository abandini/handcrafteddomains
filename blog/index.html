Domain Glossary

Exact-Match Domain (EMD)

A domain name that exactly matches a keyword or phrase that users might search for.

Brandable Domain

A unique, memorable domain name that doesn't necessarily include keywords but works well as a brand identifier.

Domain Appraisal

The process of determining the market value of a domain name based on various factors.

TLD (Top-Level Domain)

The last segment of a domain name, such as .com, .org, or .io.

Domain Aftermarket

The marketplace for buying and selling previously registered domain names.
